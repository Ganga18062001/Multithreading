 🔹 Important Points ⚡

 1️⃣ yield() pauses the currently executing thread temporarily.

 2️⃣ The thread goes from Running → Runnable state.

 3️⃣ It gives other threads of equal priority a chance to execute.

 4️⃣ It is only a hint to the scheduler, not a guarantee.
